Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged for an additional fee, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast is included with some room types and packages. Please check the room details or contact our reservations team to confirm if breakfast is included in your booking.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services for an additional fee. Please inform us of your flight details at least 24 hours prior to your arrival.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before check-in date are free of charge. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will incur a fee equivalent to one night's stay.
