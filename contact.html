Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website by using the booking form, by calling our reservations department at +1 (555) 123-4567, or by sending an email to reservations@luxuryhaven.com.

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer both self-parking and valet parking services. Self-parking is available at $25 per day, and valet parking is available at $35 per day with unlimited in and out privileges.

How far is the hotel from the airport?

Luxury Haven Hotel is located approximately 15 miles (24 km) from the International Airport, which is about a 25-30 minute drive depending on traffic conditions.

Do you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Yes, our chefs can accommodate various dietary requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific meals. Please inform us of your needs when making your reservation or upon check-in.
